Employer Attendance Line Answering

Every shift matters. Clear Line Answer provides dedicated attendance line answering so your front desk and supervisors can stay focused on operations while we capture absences, lates, and multi-day updates in real time.

What We Capture on Every Call

  • Employee full name
  • Department and shift
  • Reason for absence or late arrival
  • Expected return date and time
  • Multi-day leave details
  • Callback number

What You Receive

  • Immediate email or SMS alerts after each call
  • Time-stamped logs for HR, audits, and attendance reviews
  • Daily attendance summary reports
  • Optional safety and no-show escalation

Why Outsource Your Attendance Line?

  • Reduce front desk interruption and call congestion
  • Improve HR documentation and policy compliance
  • Support early morning, night, weekend, and holiday shifts
  • Strengthen safety and accountability across your workforce
