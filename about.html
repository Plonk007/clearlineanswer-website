About Clear Line Answer

Clear Line Answer was created to support organizations that cannot afford to miss critical calls — whether it is a 5 AM attendance line for a production shift or a family reaching out to a funeral home.

We are based in the Waterloo Region and focus on delivering a local, human-first experience with professional, consistent call handling and clear reporting.

With experience in operations, customer service, and multi-shift environments, we have built Clear Line Answer to remove the stress of attendance calls and after-hours calls from busy teams so they can focus on running their organizations.
